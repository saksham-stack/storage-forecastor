How to Prepare Your Data (Easy Way!)

🎯 In 30 Seconds

Here's the fastest way to get started:

1
Open the Streamlit app (dashboard)
2
Go to Data Builder tab
3
Click Generate Template
4
Fill in: Device ID, Days of data, Storage capacity, Date
5
Download blank CSV → Fill in Excel/Sheets
6
Upload to Forecast Lab → Get forecast! 🎉

📋 Three Methods in Data Builder

📊 Method 1: See Example Data

What it does: Shows you exactly what data looks like

Use this if: You want to understand the format before creating your own

✓ Best for: First-time users

🔨 Method 2: Generate Template

What it does: Creates a blank CSV that's ready to fill

You fill in:

  • Device ID (e.g., "my_phone")
  • Number of days (7-30 recommended)
  • Device type (heavy_user, light_user, etc.)
  • Storage capacity in GB
  • Start date

✓ Best for: Users with their own data

📥 Method 3: Download Real Example

What it does: Gives you ready-made sample data

Use this if: You want to test immediately without preparing data

✓ Best for: Quick testing

✅ Do's and ❌ Don'ts

✅ Do This

  • ✓ Use at least 7 days of data
  • ✓ Keep dates as YYYY-MM-DD
  • ✓ Make all GB values ≥ 0
  • ✓ Check used_gb ≤ capacity_gb
  • ✓ Use realistic numbers
  • ✓ Fill all columns

❌ Don't Do This

  • ✗ Use negative numbers
  • ✗ Mix date formats
  • ✗ Leave columns empty
  • ✗ Put used > capacity
  • ✗ Use old data (> 3 months)
  • ✗ Use less than 7 days
💡 Pro Tip: The template generator does most of the work for you! Just tell it how many days of data you have, and it creates the structure. Then you just fill in your storage numbers.

🚀 Example: Real Scenario

You have: Samsung phone with 512GB storage

You want: To predict storage for next 90 days

You do:

1
Open Data Builder tab in app
2
Click Generate Template
3
Fill form:
Device ID: samsung_s23 Days: 30 Profile: heavy_user Capacity: 512 Start: 2024-01-01
4
Get blank CSV with 30 rows (auto-dated)
5
Open in Excel/Sheets, fill in your actual numbers
6
Go to Forecast Lab tab
7
Upload your CSV → See predictions! 🎉
